I have been drinking this almost religiously these past couple months, and I think it is the best drink I've ever had. Here is what I put in it:

  • 4-5 cups of milk
  • 2 dates
  • 2 bannanas
  • 1/2-1 teaspoon of kewda water
I blend it all together. Depending on the ripeness of the bananas, I add less or more milk and dates. The kewda water also adds a nice hint of flavor, but it is important not to put too much and overpower everything else. I drink this every single day.
